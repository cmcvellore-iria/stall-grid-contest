Welcome to the CMC Stall Grid Passport

During the 78th IRIA Annual Conference at CMC Vellore, explore the trade area, interact with exhibitors and discover new technologies. This digital passport records each stall you visit using a simple QR scan.

How it works:

  • Register once with your email and a password.
  • Login on your phone or tablet.
  • At each exhibitor stall, tap the stall tile on your screen.
  • Scan the QR code displayed at that stall.
  • Your visit is recorded automatically in the system.
  • Watch your name climb the live leaderboard as you explore.
